Each dosing band defines the dose for a specific age group, indication, and calculation method. A drug can have multiple bands — e.g. a neonatal band (by GA) + a paediatric band (by weight), or a standard band + a high-dose band for severe infections.

Calculation methods: Weight (mg/kg · mcg/kg · units/kg · mmol/kg) · BSA (mg/m²) · Fixed dose · GFR-based (Calvert/Schwartz) · Continuous infusion (mcg/kg/min) · Age-based (neonatal GA tiers)
