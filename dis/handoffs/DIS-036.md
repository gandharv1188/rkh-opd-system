## 3. Decisions taken

### D-1: Use the heavier `tests/helpers/fake-adapters.ts` fakes

**Context:** Two fake toolsets exist — `dis/src/core/__fakes__/` (lightweight, used by `tests/unit/orchestrator.test.ts`) and `tests/helpers/fake-adapters.ts` (DIS-012 script-driven). The brief pins us to the latter.
**Decision:** Imported from `../helpers/fake-adapters.js`, script the FileRouter/Structuring entries.
**Reason:** Integration surface + CS-traceability. Script-driven fakes log calls for later inspection (unused here but available for DIS-038's audit spy).

### D-2: Key the FileRouter script on `''`, not on `'note.pdf'`

**Context:** Orchestrator.process() calls `fileRouter.route({ filename: '' })` (orchestrator.ts:161) regardless of the originally ingested filename.
**Decision:** Script the fake on the empty-string key and document it inline.
**Reason:** Matches real behaviour. First run failed on `no script entry for ` — confirming the orchestrator does not thread the filename through `process()`. This is a known DRIFT item in the orchestrator but out of scope here.

### D-3: Assert the persisted row is in `ready_for_review` AND `!== auto_approved`

**Reason:** CS-7 is stated as "disabled ⇒ no auto-promotion". Two negative assertions harden the test against future refactors that might introduce an `auto_approved` target state.
