# DIS-151 — Clinician weekly audit dry-run (CS-6)

## Scope

Synthetic weekly audit batch (20 extractions, 3 operators, approve/reject mix)
with acceptance tests asserting the audit trail carries everything needed for
the CS-6 weekly clinician sign-off: operator attribution on both approvals and
rejections, ISO timestamps, confidence summary per extraction, and operator
coverage.

## Files

- `dis/tests/clinical-acceptance/weekly-audit.test.ts` — 5 acceptance tests
- `dis/tests/fixtures/clinical-audit/README.md` — fixture dataset doc

## CS-6 batched sign-off

Weekly audit dry-run establishes the schema + coverage expectations for the
clinician's weekly sign-off session: every extraction that flows into the
review queue must carry operator_id, signed_at, and confidence_summary; a
healthy week must span at least 3 operators (no single-point-of-failure). The
synthetic generator can be swapped for a real-DB extractor in Wave 6 without
changing the test contract.
